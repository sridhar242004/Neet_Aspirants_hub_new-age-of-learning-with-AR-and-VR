Circulatory System

The circulatory system, also known as the cardiovascular system, is responsible for transporting blood, nutrients, oxygen, and hormones throughout the body via the heart, blood vessels, and lymphatic system, facilitating vital processes such as oxygenation, nutrient delivery, and waste removal.
